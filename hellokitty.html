Hello Kitty

Shinkansen

The Hello Kitty Shinkansen debuted at the end of June 2018 and still runs to dis day. It travels between Shin-Osaka and Hakata Station in western Japan's Fukuoka prefecture, making stops at 19 stations. It's speed is approximetly 300km/h but it rarely reaches it, due to the frequent stops.

Hello Plaza Room

This is the most unique car on the train, and perhaps of any Shinkansen! There are no passenger seats in this first car, but instead, it is completely dedicated to Hello Kitty merchandise and fun. There are Hello Kitty displays and photo ops, and even a small Hello Kitty theatre. There is a rest area to sit and enjoy your Hello Kitty snacks. But the main attraction is the shop, with limited edition Hello Kitty merchandise special to each region featured in Western Japan.

Kawaii! Room

All passenger cars in the Hello Kitty Shinkansen are appointed with purple or pink seats; white headrest covers with the train’s namesake, and pink and white flooring full of Hello Kitty bowties and other motifs. However, the walls of the train are otherwise grey and plain. But this is not the case in the Kawaii! Room, where Hello Kitty characters are plastered all over the walls. The baggage storage area is adorned with numerous Hello Kitty illustrations.

Must Buy Souveniers

The Hello Kitty Shinkansen sells exclusive, limited-edition Hello Kitty merch, available only on board. We've picked some of the most popular items you shouldn’t miss during your journey on this pink high-speed train.

Food

Ekiben, lunch boxes enjoyed on the shinkansen, are an essential part of the train travel experience in Japan. Before boarding the pink bullet train, make sure to pick up a Hello Kitty-themed bento box at the ekiben shops inside the stations.The meal is placed inside a miniature version of the Hello Kitty Shinkansen, featuring a small hamburger steak, sausage, fried egg, chicken rice, Hello Kitty-themed kamaboko fish cake, and a jelly dessert. The container is made from plastic and reusable, making it a great souvenir.
