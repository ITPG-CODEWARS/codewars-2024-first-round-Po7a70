The train

Starting on Thursday, November 1, 2018, which is also Hello Kitty's birthday, a fully wrapped train with a cute pink base and designs of flowers and popular Sanrio characters is in operation. The cars feature all 10 pf sanrio's characters.The train's start is at the Keio Tama Center Station.

1-5th car

First car features helloKitty holding a tulip. Secon car is the Pochacco character holding red sweet peas in his hands. Third car has the little Twin Stars surrownded by colourfull pansies. Fourth car is Cinnamoroll holding a bouquet of colourfull flowers. And lastly the fifth car features multiple sanrio characters such as Pochacco, Pom Pom Purin, Hello Kitty, Bad Badtz-Maru, and Kero Kero Keroppi.

6-10th car

The last five cars feature the rest of the sanrio characters. Sixt car is Pom Pom Purin with an orange ribbon around his ear. The seventh car has a cute design of Kero Kero Keroppi holding hydrangeas in both hands. Bad Badtz-Maru holds a single crimson rose on the eight car. Caracters Little Twin Stars (Kiki & Lala), Cinnamoroll, and My Melody are all around the ninth car. And lastly the tenth car has MyMelody with a cute dasy flower decoration.

The inside

The Sanrio Pularoid express has a lot of attention to detail even inside the train.The warning stickers on the doors have the same design as the train body, creating a sense of unity.You can enjoy the train not only from the outside, but also from the inside.

Station Decorations

Since March 12, 2016, Hello Kitty has also been the honorary stationmaster of Keio Tama Center Station, watching over the safety of visitors.In front of the ticket gate, there is a gorgeous glass-style ceiling lighting. Next to the ticket booth is a spot introducing the characters who work as station staff! Hello Kitty signs will show you your destination! She also appears on the clock on the platform. The waiting room is designed with Hello Kitty's ribbon. The theme song of Sanrio Puroland, "Puro March" is used as the train approach melody.

Train Departure Ceremony

As the train entered the platform, the venue was filled with smiles at the adorable train, and the honorary stationmaster, Hello Kitty, and the Keio Tama Center Stationmaster, posed with the words "All aboard!" as the train departed for the next station
